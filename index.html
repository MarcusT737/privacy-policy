Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gothenlabs ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Budget App mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information you provide directly:

  • Account data: email address, optional first and last name
  • Financial data: income records and expenditure records you enter
  • Device preferences: app theme and language (stored locally on your device)

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Budget App service — authenticating your account and storing your financial records so you can access them across sessions.

3. Data Storage

Account and financial data is stored on a secure cloud server. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Local preferences are stored on your device only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your data — available within the app (Profile screen)
  • Export your data — use the CSV export in the Summary screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data — available in Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account

For any other requests, contact us at marcus.thenander@gothenlabs.com.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete your account. Upon deletion, all data is permanently removed from our servers.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at marcus.thenander@gothenlabs.com.
